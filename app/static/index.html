Normalize genres & fill album artists.

Complete genres
Give every artist one consistent genre.
Proposes a genre per artist from the curated map, the majority of existing tags, then optionally MusicBrainz. Review and edit before applying; a sole Holiday tag is preserved.

Analyze BPM & energy
Enables tempo/energy playlist rules.
Detects each track's tempo and a 0–100 energy score. Slow (~1–2s/track) but resumable — safe to run overnight.
5
Convert → iPod
Build the AAC mirror for iPod/iTunes.
Transcodes FLAC to a 256k AAC mirror; MP3/AAC copied as-is, DRM skipped. Source is never modified and re-runs only convert what's missing.
